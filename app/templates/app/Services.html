Our team offers comprehensive 40-year recertification services essential for ensuring the ongoing safety and compliance of your property. Reserve Studies

Our civil engineering team is proficient in conducting comprehensive Condominium Reserve Studies, which are essential for the long-term financial planning and maintenance of condominium properties. We meticulously evaluate the condition of common elements and major components within your condominium, such as roofing, HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical infrastructure, and exterior facades. By projecting the future repair and replacement costs, we help you establish a realistic and effective reserve funding plan. Our detailed reports provide valuable insights that assist in informed decision-making, ensuring the sustained integrity, safety, and value of your property.
